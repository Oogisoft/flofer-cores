About

A family-run local business since 2006

Built on local relationships, honest work and years of experience in parts, salvage and recycling.

Flofer Cores is a family-run parts, salvage and recycling business serving Chicago and the greater Chicagoland area since 2006. Built on local relationships, honest work and years of experience, we help people find usable parts, clear out salvageable materials and keep valuable items in circulation.

We know the area and the local network. We come across used parts quickly, we’re usually reachable by phone or text, and we’d rather give you a straight answer than make a big sales pitch. That’s the way we’ve always done it.
