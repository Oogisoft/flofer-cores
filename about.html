About

A father-and-son business with Guatemalan roots and Chicago history.

Flofer Cores is run by Fernando Sr. and Fernando Jr., known as Chito. They have been doing local parts, salvage and recycling work since 2006.

Fernando Sr. and Chito are Guatemalan and have lived in Chicago their whole lives. Flofer Cores reflects both sides of that story: practical Chicago work ethic, close family roots and a direct way of doing business.

The work is not fancy. It is knowing where parts move, what still has value and who to call. They help people find used parts, clear salvageable materials and keep usable items in circulation.

If you call or text, you are reaching the family behind the business. They will tell you what they can do, what they cannot do and what information they need next.
