Shop & Business Cleanouts

Select shop and business cleanouts

For local shops, garages and businesses with parts, metal, equipment or salvageable inventory to clear.

Flofer Cores helps with select cleanouts when the space includes materials that still have value. That may mean old parts, metal, tools, equipment or inventory that can be recovered or recycled.

This is not general trash hauling. We are best for shops, garages and business spaces where salvage is part of the job.
