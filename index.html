Local. Honest. Practical.

Nearly 20 years of local parts and salvage know-how

For nearly 20 years, Flofer Cores has been built on local relationships, practical service and honest work. Our inventory changes often, but our approach stays the same: help people find what they need, recover what still has value and recycle what we can.

We’re more parts-oriented than junk-removal — we come across used auto parts quickly through local routes and shop connections, and we’re usually reachable by phone or text.
