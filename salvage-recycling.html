Salvage Pickup & Recycling

Salvage pickup & recycling across Chicagoland

Have parts, scrap, equipment or salvageable materials taking up space? We handle select pickups throughout Chicago and the greater Chicagoland area.

Have parts, scrap metal, equipment, appliances or other salvageable materials taking up space? Flofer Cores handles select pickups throughout Chicago and the greater Chicagoland area. Whenever possible, we recover, resell or recycle materials instead of sending them straight to disposal.

We focus on items that still have value. If you’re not sure whether what you’ve got is a fit, send a couple of photos and where you’re located — we’ll tell you straight.
